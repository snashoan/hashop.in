# Hashop fast local commerce
App About

Privacy & rules

Only what commerce needs.

Hashop stores operational data for accounts, shops, listings, support, and orders. It is not built as a consumer attention machine.

Open shops About Hashop
Guest buying stays possible. Payments can stay direct. Fork rules can change.

Stored data

  • Buyer. Saved name, contact, address, and local order references.
  • Shop. Listing, stock, location, contact, payment notes, and owner access.
  • Order. Buyer snapshot, cart items, delivery or pickup note, status, and payment mode.
  • Support. Account notes used to debug issues without a separate desk.

Verification and payment

Contact verification proves the account can be reached. Hashop does not need card vaulting for the core flow; shops can use direct payment, pay on receive, or their own payment setup.

Operating rules

  • Shops. Accuracy, fulfillment, refunds, taxes, and legal goods.
  • Buyers. Correct contact, address, pickup, and delivery information.
  • Operators. Remove illegal, abusive, deceptive, or harmful listings.
  • Forks. Self-hosted copies may change auth, hosting, support, and retention.
