# Hashop fast local commerce
App Privacy

Hashop objective

Find. Order. Leave.

Hashop is built for short consumer sessions: open nearby shops, check what exists, place the order, and get out.

Open shops Set account
Browse without login. Shop owners list stock. No feed to babysit.

Commerce primitive

Hashop is buyer first, but shop ready. A buyer can stay guest, save account permanence when useful, and later extend the same account into a shop.

Operating shape

  • Buyer. Name, contact, address only when permanence matters.
  • Shop. Location, listings, stock, contact, and payment choices.
  • Order. The transaction record, not a content loop.
  • Fork. Replace auth, verification, and hosting without changing the model.

What Hashop refuses

We are not trying to keep consumers scrolling. The product is successful when a buyer finishes in minutes and the shop gets a clean order.
